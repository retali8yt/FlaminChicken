About Us

Our Story

Flamin’ Chicken began with Mr Sheibani who after years of working at KFC founded his own family-run chicken business in 1996 based in Rochdale, Lancashire eventually expanding into the North West & the rest of the UK.

From the start it has been about fresh ingredients a secret West African spice mix and peri peri flame grilling that keeps taste and tradition alive. Unlike corporate chains Flamin’ Chicken has stayed proudly independent grown through family dedication not franchised by big business.  At the heart of it all is Mehran the Delivery Driver our national animal, ensuring every order is hot fresh and arrives exactly when it should. Flamin’ Chicken - family run since day one - spiced across generations - delivered by Mehran...

Our Vision

Our vision at Flamin Chicken is to be the most trusted family run chicken business known for exceptional quality and flavour. We aim to grow while keeping our values strong delivering fresh tasty chicken with care and passion. We want every customer to feel like part of our family and to enjoy the same authentic experience that has been passed down through generations. We are committed to innovation and excellence while staying true to our roots and supporting our community.
